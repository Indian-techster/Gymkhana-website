About

       

       We came, we saw
We conqured

LNMIIT Quiz Club was founded with the sole motive to inculcate quizzing culture in our Institute. It started as an independent body but was later introduced in the Science & Tech. Council. The club was started by 3 odd members, and today it is a forty member organization.

We have represented LNMIIT in numerous quiz competitions and have won most of them. Winning a quiz has been a cakewalk for us, may it be our inter college quiz or Vivacity or at any other college in Jaipur. Our Performances at BITS Pilani –India Quiz, TATA Crucible, IIM Lucknow’s Business Quiz are few of our remarkable achievements.

We have successfully organized many quizzing events during Plinth and Vivacity which were a big success.In year 2015 we had Mr. Kunal Savarkar(KBC Content head) as a host in our quiz fest. In 2011 we started our FB Group and page where we took quizzing to a new level and we are getting enormous response from all the parts of the country. Our club was infact the first club to make its own facebook page.

We came, we saw, we conqured.
