About

              

"Fashion is in the sky, in the street. Fashion has to do with ideas,the way we live, what is happening. "
-Coco Channel

By living up to every essence of Fashion; be it the 'La Mode' outfits, eye-popping ideas and creative designs, Eminence believes that fashion is not superficial but comes from within.
Here, with us one learns the art of carrying himself in style and elegance. No bars-No rules-no Expectation. Passion is the only key to us. Eminence invites everybody to explore one's own style and become a part of this enthralling experience.
