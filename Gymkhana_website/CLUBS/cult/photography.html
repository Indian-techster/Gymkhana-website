About

              

Photography is truth. The cinema is truth twenty-four times per second

Imagination is the in-house photography club of LNMIIT. We're a bunch of visual enthusiasts that go around practicing and encouraging the art. The members of the club engage in various activities governed by lightplay. Presently we focus on the disciplines of Photography and Cinematography. The club also aids in covering different events that happen in and around the campus.
