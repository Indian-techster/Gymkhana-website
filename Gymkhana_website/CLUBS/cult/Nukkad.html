Acheivements And Work

This year’s LNMIIT Nukkad Mandli in its attempt to raise our voice against this mayhem performed three different street plays on various social issues: “Sab Chalta Hai”Performed on the institute’s foundation day, this play showcased the nonchalant and permissive attitude of the majority people in our country. “Khoonchala”Performed in Rendezvous’14- Cultural Fest of IIT-D and in IRIS'15-Cultural Fest of IIM-Indore where we bagged 2nd Prize, this play creates awareness regarding the need and benefits of donating blood. “Jaativaad ka Vivaad”Performed in Vivacity’15 , Renissance’15 –Cultural Fest of JECRC and Vivacity'16 where we bagged 1 St And 2nd Prize respectively.
