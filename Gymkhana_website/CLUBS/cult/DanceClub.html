About

              

The college has an enthusiastic dance club that has an established dance crew namely "INSIGNIA".

The crew has brought laurels to the college. All the events where the college is to be represented, members of the dance team perform who are selected through auditions that are held for every session. There are intra college events like Freshers, DPL (Dance Premier League) and Nostalgia where the participation is open to all . The dance club encourages dedicated work and participation from all .
