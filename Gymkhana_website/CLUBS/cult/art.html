About

              

One of the most active clubs of the Cultural Council,

Creativity is what the Art Club nurtures. It intensifies the beauty of normality into something appreciable. One of the most active clubs of the Cultural Council, Art Club organizes art events throughout the session to bring out the talent in every possible way. From displaying artistic talent to decorating the college for the fests and events to bring the set aura, there isn't a moment when art club fails to showcase its talent.
