Carrom

Carrom is one of the most active sports in college.

Carrom is played using small disks of Wood or Plastic known as carrom men, sometimes abbreviated c/m. The pieces are also known as seed, coin or Pawnpuck.

College provides well maintained carrom inventory and has a separate carrom room in SAC.
