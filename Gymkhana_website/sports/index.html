The LNMIIT Sports Council

The sports council mananges all the sports clubs in college. It is responsible for managing all the college teams in all sports. The council also takes all decisions with regards to sports infrastructure of college and sports inventory.

Council also organizes an Annual Internation Sports Fest called Desportivos.

Sports council is also responsible for organizing various intercollege and intracollege sports activities and helps establish and promote positive sports culture in college.

There are 12 types of sports in LNMIIT

Cricket, Football, Basketball, Volleyball, Table Tennis, Squash, Badminton, Lawn Tennis, Chess, Carrom, Karate, Taekwando.
